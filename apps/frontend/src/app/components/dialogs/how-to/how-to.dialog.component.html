How To

Each player has four buttons - blue, yellow, purple, and red. A big label in the middle of the screen displays the name of one of these colors. The background color of this label is randomly chosen among the other colors. The players have to press the button matching the word (not the background color). Hitting the right button earns one point. Wrong clicks loose 2 points. The game ends after 2 minutes and the player with the highest score wins.
